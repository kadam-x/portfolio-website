# Self-taught Dev | Finance & CS Student

I am a 23-year-old finance and semi-CS student, but most importantly a self-taught developer. I enjoy simple and clean but blazingly fast design. Always had a systems thinking wanting to optimize to the last nanosecond. I truly believe in order to understand something you first have to struggle by building it from scratch. Once I finish my BSc I'll be doing a my masters in CS, specializing in low latency computing. My main interest lies in quantitative research, systematic trading, and turning financial theory into code that can be tested, measured, and broken. I am currently learning C++ and Rust, with a focus on performance, low-level control, and writing faster research tooling. This site is my workspace made public — small experiments, side projects, notes, and ideas that reflect what I am currently curious about.
